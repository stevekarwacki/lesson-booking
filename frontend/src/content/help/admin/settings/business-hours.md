# Set Business Hours

Business hours define when your business operates each day of the week. Instructors can only set their availability within these hours, and students cannot book outside them.

## Where to find it

> Settings → Business Information (scroll down to the Business Hours section)

## Steps

1. For each day, toggle it **Open** or **Closed**.
2. For open days, set the opening and closing times.
3. Click **Save Business Information**.

## Notes

- Different hours per day are supported (e.g., shorter hours on Fridays).
- If you narrow your hours after instructors have already set availability, any instructor slots outside the new hours will be hidden automatically. The data is not deleted — widening the hours again restores them.
- Changes take effect immediately for new bookings.
