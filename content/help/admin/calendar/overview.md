# Calendar

The Calendar page is your operational view of the day. It has two sections: a **Today's Bookings** panel at the top and a **7-day schedule grid** below.

## Where to find it

> Calendar (top navigation)

## Today's Bookings panel

The panel at the top of the page shows every lesson scheduled for today. It updates automatically when you book, reschedule, or cancel a lesson. If no lessons are scheduled, it shows **"No bookings found."** with a link to the full Bookings page.

To narrow the panel to a single instructor, use the instructor search at the top of the page.

## Schedule grid

The 7-day grid below the panel shows the full availability and booking picture for the selected instructor. Green slots are available to book; blue slots are already booked.

→ [Navigate the calendar grid](navigate-calendar.md)
→ [Book a lesson on behalf of a student](book-on-behalf.md)

## Switching instructors

Use the **instructor search** at the top of the page to switch to a different instructor. The page title, Today's Bookings panel, and schedule grid all update to reflect that instructor.

## Need the full booking history?

The Calendar page only shows today. For a searchable, filtered view of all lessons across dates and instructors, go to **Bookings** in the top navigation.
