# Calendar and Bookings

The Calendar page shows a weekly view of all lessons for any instructor. As an admin you can book, reschedule, and view lessons directly from here.

## Navigating the calendar

Use the instructor selector and week arrows to browse any instructor's schedule. Each booked slot shows the student's name and lesson duration. Greyed-out slots are blocked by Google Calendar (if configured).

## Booking a lesson

Click any available slot to open the booking modal. You select the student, confirm the payment method, and book — the student doesn't need to do anything.

→ [Book a lesson on behalf of a student](book-on-behalf.md)

## Rescheduling

Click an existing booking to open its detail view, where you can move it to a different slot.

→ [Reschedule a student's lesson](reschedule-booking.md)

## Cancelling and refunds

Cancellations and refunds are handled from the student's **Bookings** tab in the Users page, not from the calendar directly.

→ [Cancel a student's lesson](cancel-booking.md)
→ [Process a refund for a lesson](process-refund.md)
