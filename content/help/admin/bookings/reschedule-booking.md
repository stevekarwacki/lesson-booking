# Reschedule a Student's Lesson

Move an existing booking to a different date or time. You can do this from the Bookings page, the Calendar, or the student's profile.

## From the Bookings page

1. Go to **Bookings** and find the lesson using the filters.
2. Click **Reschedule** on the booking.
3. Pick a new date and available time slot.
4. Click **Confirm Reschedule**.

## From the Calendar

1. Go to **Calendar** and find the booking (it appears as a blue slot).
2. Click the slot to open the booking detail.
3. Pick a new date and available time slot.
4. Click **Confirm Reschedule**.

## From a student's profile

1. Go to **Users** → find the student → **Manage** → **Bookings** tab.
2. Find the lesson and click **Reschedule**.
3. Pick a new date and time slot.
4. Click **Confirm Reschedule**.

## Notes

- Only available time slots (within the instructor's availability and not already booked) are shown.
- The lesson duration is preserved — a 60-minute booking reschedules as 60 minutes.
- Recurring subscription lessons cannot be rescheduled individually. Those are managed through the student's recurring booking settings.
- Admins are not subject to the 24-hour modification window that applies to students.
