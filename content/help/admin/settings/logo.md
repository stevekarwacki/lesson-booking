# Upload or Change Your Logo

Add your company logo to the navigation header. It also appears in outgoing emails.

## Where to find it

> Settings → Theme & Branding (Logo section)

## Upload a logo

1. Click **Choose File** and select an image (JPG, PNG, WebP, or GIF).
2. The logo is processed and uploaded automatically — no save button needed.

**Size requirements:** minimum 50×50px, maximum 400×100px, file size under 5MB. Images are resized automatically if they exceed the maximum.

## Set logo position

Choose where the logo appears in the navigation header:

- **Left** — logo sits beside the company name on the left
- **Center** — logo is centered, company name stays on the left

The position saves immediately when you select it.

## Remove the logo

Click **Remove Logo**. The logo is deleted from the server and the company name is shown in its place.

See also: [Customize your theme and colors](theme-and-branding.md)
