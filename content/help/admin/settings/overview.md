# Settings

Settings is organised into six tabs. Changes take effect immediately unless noted otherwise.

## Tabs at a glance

| Tab | What you configure |
|---|---|
| **Business Information** | Company name, contact details, address, social links, and weekly business hours |
| **Theme & Branding** | Colour palette, custom colours, and your logo |
| **Lesson Settings** | Default lesson duration and payment policies |
| **Email Settings** | How the system sends email, and email template customisation |
| **Calendar Settings** | Google Calendar integration for blocking instructor availability |
| **Advanced Settings** | File storage configuration |
